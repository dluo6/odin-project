Mango Sorbet

Description

This mango sorbet recipe only takes 15 minutes to whip up and yields 12 servings, or 6 cups. See the original webiste on Allrecipes here.

Ingredients

  • Mango: 4 mangos - peeled, seeded, and cubed.
  • Simple syrup: 1 cup.
  • Lime juice: 3 tablespoons fresh lime juice.

Steps

  1. Put mangos into a food processor and puree. Add the simple syrup and lime juice and continuine to puree until smooth.
  2. Place in an ice cream maker, and freeze thoroughly.
