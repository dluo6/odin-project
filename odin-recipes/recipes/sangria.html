Sangria

Description

Sangria is an alcoholic cocktail originating from Spain and Portgual. It consists of wine, liquuer, sparkling water, fruit juice, and fruit slices. Although there are red and white sangria, this recipe uses red wine. With a total time of 30 minutes split evenly between prepping and cooking, this recipe is quick to prepare and yields 6 servings. See the original website on Allrecipes here.

Ingredients

  • Red wine: 1 750 ml bottle of dry red wine.
  • Brandy: 1/2 cup.
  • Triple Sec: 1/2 cup.
  • Fruit juice: 1/3 cup orange juice, 1/4 cup lemon juice, and 1/3 cup frozen lemon concentrate.
  • Sugar (optional): 1/4 cup white sugar.
  • Carbonated water (optional): 2 cups.
  • Fruit: 1 medium orange, 1 medium lime, and 1 medium lemon all sliced into rounds and 8 maraschino cherries.

Steps

See the website for tips on success.

  1. Gather ingredients.
  2. Mix red wine, brancy, triple sec, fruit juice, and sugar in a bowl. Add all fruit into the bowl.
  3. Serve over ice or refrigerate 8 hours for best flavor. Add carbonated water just before serving for fizziness.
